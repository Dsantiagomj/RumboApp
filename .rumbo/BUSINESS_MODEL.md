## üéì Key Metrics to Track

### Acquisition:

- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** Cost to acquire 1 user
- **Target:** <$5 per user (via organic + content)

### Engagement:

- **DAU/MAU:** Daily active / Monthly active users
- **Target:** 40%+ (users engage 12+ days per month)
- **AI chat sessions:** Average 3-5 per week

### Retention:

- **30-day retention:** % still using after 30 days
- **Target:** 60%+
- **90-day retention:** % still using after 90 days
- **Target:** 40%+

### Conversion:

- **Free ‚Üí Pro conversion:** % upgrading to paid
- **Target:** 15-20%
- **Upgrade timeline:** Average days from signup to upgrade
- **Target:** <30 days

### Revenue:

- **MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue):** Total subscription revenue/month
- **ARPU (Average Revenue Per User):** MRR / total users
- **Target:** $1.50-2.00/user (mix of free + paid)
- **LTV (Lifetime Value):** Total revenue from average user
- **Target:** $100+ (2-3 years retention)

### Product:

- **Receipts scanned:** Total receipts uploaded
- **Target:** 50%+ of Pro users scan weekly
- **Plans created:** Users with active financial plans
- **Target:** 70%+ of Pro users
- **Tasks completed:** From AI-generated plans
- **Target:** 60%+ task completion rate

## üîí Ethical Considerations

### Data Privacy:

- ‚úÖ Never sell user data (ever)
- ‚úÖ Don't train public AI models on user data
- ‚úÖ Encrypt sensitive info at rest & in transit
- ‚úÖ Allow data export (GDPR-like)
- ‚úÖ Allow account deletion

### AI Recommendations:

- ‚úÖ Transparent about why we suggest things
- ‚úÖ No paid placements (stores don't pay for recommendations)
- ‚úÖ User's best interest always first
- ‚úÖ Disclose limitations (AI can be wrong)

### Pricing:

- ‚úÖ Free tier is genuinely useful (not crippled)
- ‚úÖ No dark patterns to force upgrades
- ‚úÖ Clear pricing, no surprise fees
- ‚úÖ Easy to downgrade or cancel
