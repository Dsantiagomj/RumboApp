## ğŸ¤– AI Philosophy

### How AI Works in Rumbo:

#### **1. Learning Mode**

AI observes without being intrusive:

- Learns spending patterns
- Identifies habits (grocery frequency, favorite stores)
- Understands financial state
- Maps goals and priorities

#### **2. Predictive Mode**

AI anticipates needs:

- "You'll run out of X soon"
- "This expense is unusual for you"
- "You're trending toward overspending"

#### **3. Recommendation Mode**

AI suggests improvements:

- "Buy here, not thereâ€”save $X"
- "Switch to this productâ€”better for your budget/health"
- "Allocate $X to savings this month"

#### **4. Coaching Mode**

AI holds you accountable:

- Daily check-ins
- Reminder nudges
- Progress updates
- Encouragement and celebration

#### **5. Planning Mode**

AI creates actionable plans:

- Financial improvement roadmap
- Debt payoff strategy
- Savings goals with timeline
- Task breakdown

### AI Behavior:

- **Voice:** Supportive coach, not judgmental accountant
- **Proactivity:** Offers help, doesn't overwhelm
- **Personalization:** Adapts to YOUR situation
- **Transparency:** Explains WHY it suggests things
- **Privacy:** Data stays secure, never shared
